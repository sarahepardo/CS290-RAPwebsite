Birthday Ideas for this Special PUP

Bake a dog-friendly treat or cake. There’s no better way to show your puppy some
love on their first birthday than to bake them a delicious treat!

Throw a puppy birthday party. Birthday parties aren’t just for humans—puppies can
definitely partake in the fun! Share yummy puppy treats and cakes with all fur-babies.

Give your pup a new toy. Make your pup’s first birthday a happy one by giving them a
brand new toy. Your puppy will love it!

Pamper and spoil your pup. An easy way to make your puppy’s birthday extra special is by
spoiling and pampering them. By simply giving them your undivided attention and love during their
birthday, your pup’s mood will instantly improve.

Take your pup to a dog-friendly park or restaurant. Taking your puppy out on an adventure helps
make their birthday extra special. Look for nearby dog-friendly parks and take them out for a long walk.

Snap a cute photo of you and your pup. Commemorate your pup’s first birthday with an adorable
photo of you and your puppy.
